About company


iDevices - official partner of Apple in the Republic of Kazakhstan with the status of Apple Premium Reseller.


Apple Premium Reseller status is given only to professionals in their field.

In our stores, you can not only buy an iPhone, Mac computer or iPad tablet, but also other Apple equipment and accessories. iDevices is, first of all, a place saturated with the atmosphere of Apple, where they will help you with the choice of technology, teach you how to use it, give advice on using your gadgets and offer quality accessories.

At iDevices, you can work with any Mac, iPhone or iPad, watch demos of recently launched Apple products, or take part in trainings, workshops, and events dedicated to the latest technology. We know everything about Apple products. And we are ready to share our knowledge.

Our salespeople are real experts in their field. And high-quality, convenient, easy-to-use Apple technology gives pleasure to its users and requires virtually no technical support.

In our store, you can always buy whatever you need.
